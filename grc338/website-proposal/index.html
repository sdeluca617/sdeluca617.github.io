Final project proposal

Introduction

Winter Chill

Winter Chill is a small local ice cream shop with a wide variety of flavors. It is self-managed by a group of friends with a passion for rmaking ice cream!

Target audience

Anyone who loves ice cream and wants a quick dessert.

Their goal is to scope out places to by ice cream and see if any of the flavors interest them so they can stop buy and purchase a scoop.
